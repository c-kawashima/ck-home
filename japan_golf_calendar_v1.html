Kanagawa Prefecture — Club Recommendation

Best option for a 4th club to track, accessible from Yokohama

🏆 Primary Recommendation: 相模原ゴルフクラブ (Sagamihara Golf Club)

LocationSagamihara City, Kanagawa Prefecture
Websitesagamihara-gc.co.jp
CoursesEast + West (36 holes)
From Yokohama~35km / ~50 min
Why this club: Sagamihara GC hosts more KNGA (Kanagawa Golf Association) championship events than any other club in the prefecture — the Men's Final, Women's Final, City/Town Tournament, and Amateur semifinals are all held here. It's the unofficial "headquarters" of competitive Kanagawa golf. 36-hole layout means it can run large field events simultaneously.

Alternative: レイクウッドゴルフクラブ (Lakewood Golf Club)

LocationChigasaki City / Ninomiya-machi, Kanagawa
CoursesEast + West courses
Why relevant for juniors: Lakewood (West course) hosts the KNGA Junior Championship — all age groups including girls 12–14 and girls 15–17. If tracking junior competitions in Kanagawa, this is the key venue.
